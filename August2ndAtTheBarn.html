Please join us Saturday August 2nd, 2025 at the Tango Barn!

This is our premier event of the summer, and we are very proud to be bringing these amazing artists to the Seattle Tango Community.

Please help Seattle and the Tango Barn extend Winnie Cheung Sextet and their amazing group of musicians a warm welcome Saturday, August 2nd, 2025!

  • Music and dancing 8:00 pm - 11:30 pm by Winnie Cheung, accompanied by 4 Bandoneons, Violin, Cello, Double Bass and a very special guest from Argentina playing the piano! Greg Constantino will be DJ.
  • Pre-Milonga lesson taught by so very talented Katia Diamond from San Diego and Andrea Moncayo from Tijuana at 6:30 pm - 7:30 pm with a Performance at intermission by Katia Diamond and Andrea Moncayo
  • Class is $20. Milonga is $40. Take both for $50. The teachers are wonderful!
  • Admission is limited to 120 guests so please RSVP early to reserve your space. When emailing, please specify your party size. We are offering tables by reservation: TheTangoBarn@gmail.com. There will be some chairs behind tables for general seating.
  • In search of 4-6 fun people for volunteer. Reach out to The Tango Barn - Includes free entrada!
    • Sign-in Person
    • Hostess to seat people at reserved tables
    • Food and drink helper 1 & 2 at Milonga
    • We could use 1 person to help with filling the water and sweep up after the Milonga. Takes about 20 minutes!

Thanks so much for your continued support. We realize $50 might be a little high for some people's budgets but we need to collect the extra funds to fly both sets of musicians to Seattle.
